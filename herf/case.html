What we learn from this case?

There is an interesting point that this Youtuber mentioned in this video--"The idea of clinical decline complicated by an incomplete patient history has always been a problem in medical practice."Especially in the two examples in the video at 7:53to10:09, the doctor did not know the patient’s previous case and led to a misdiagnosis. Examples like this are countless in our lives. Not only have to pay more medical expenses, but it may also delay the illness. With the electronic medical card, doctors can understand the patient’s previous condition. This will not only solve the above-mentioned problems, but will also greatly increase the efficiency of the hospital. I hope that this mode of medical treatment can spread to hospitals across the country. So that people will be diagnosed with accuracy and reliability.
